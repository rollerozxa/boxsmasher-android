Box Smasher is a physics-based puzzle game where you shoot balls to smash structures of boxes off the screen and into the void. The objective is to clear each level of all boxes in a limited amount of balls. Shoot balls to smash boxes out into the void! Do you have what it takes to clear them all in the amount of balls given?

Currently the game has 17 levels, with more coming soon. Do you have what it takes to complete them all?
